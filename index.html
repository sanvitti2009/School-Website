school achievements

INTER SCHOOL POETRY RECITATION COMPETITION
Won second in inter Poetry Recitation

St.Mary’s School conducted an Inter school Poetry Recitation competition on 12th December 2014 . The students were supposed to recite a self composed poem .The topics given for the poem were – Love Jihad or Swachta Abhiyan. The Hindi Department of Delhi Public School sent Pallavi Chauhan of class 11 N who bagged the second position and won a cash prize of Rs.1000 and a certificate.

MOSAIC INTER-SCHOOL FEST 2020
Won first price in inter school online 'Mosiac 2020' competition

Dhriti Tandon of class VIII-D DPS brought laurels to the school by bagging the First Position in an online Inter-School Competition ‘Mosaic-2020’ organized by DPS Saharanpur on 10th December, 2020. She participated in the event ‘Ad Mad’and won every one’s heart with her comically impactful presentation on ‘Corona Alert Glasses’, a product that detects corona virus and alerts the person wearing the pair of glasses.

What's New

  • New School Canteen

    The food options and responsibilities to consider, canteens must be better organised and more efficient than they have been in the past. Online menus with photographs of the meals prepared, and detailed descriptions of their ingredients help both students and parents make the right nutritional choices. Each student or staff member who accesses the canteen could be provided with a School Canteen Account on the school website that keeps track of their previous meal choices and any dietary requirements they may have. Ordering online by ticking a box and paying for meals online can eliminate all the fuss of money handling, making it safer and less complicated for staff and students alike. Here again, diversity is the key.

  • Implemented Bus Tracking System

    It’s only natural for parents to worry about their children anytime they’re out of sight. When the school bus doesn’t arrive on time, it’s hard not to imagine the worst.
    GPS tracking software gives you the ability to monitor your buses and drivers on a map in real time. If a bus gets stuck or breaks down, you’ll know about it immediately, so you can help resolve the problem and get the bus back on track as soon as possible.
